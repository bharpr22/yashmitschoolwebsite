Policies

ATTENDANCE
Regular Attendance is necessary. Every student must qualify for 70% and more attendance during the entire academic year. Students who have been absent from school must bring a written note signed by the parents in the diary, explaining the reason for absence, on the very next day.
If a student continues to be absent for one calendar month without notice, his/her name is liable to be removed from the school register. Application for leave, for urgent or special reasons must be made in writing to the Vice Principal/ Headmistress. But the Principal has the right to reject it.
Pupils absent on the last day of term or on the first day of a new term are liable to be struck off the rolls unless prior permission has been granted.
No child will be permitted to leave the school, during school hours except in case of dire emergency or illness. In case of illness the School should be informed as soon as possible. No child will be allowed to resume classes without a letter and a medical certificate.
A pupil who has been absent from the School without sanctioned leave will not be allowed to attend classes till the Head of the School gives permission.
Non-observance of these rules will be regarded as a serious breach of discipline and may result in the pupil’s name being removed from the rolls. Students must be present in the school after holidays / vacations on the re-opening of the school. They must also remain present in the school on the last working day of the school, preceding the holidays / vacation. Absence without prior sanctions renders the pupil liable to have his/her name struck of the roll.
EXAMINATIONS
Students absent from any examination for any reason will be re-examined, only for compulsory subjects immediately after the Scheduled Exam is completed. They are promoted or detained according to the year’s work. Decision of the Examination Committee will be final.
Students found absenting themselves from the examination for unjustified reason are given no marks and marked absent.
Promotion from one standard to another will be made at the end of the school year, based on the student’s performance during all the examinations and tests.
A student will not be eligible to appear for the final academic examination if s/he fails to get adequate attendance for the year. Attendance of either of parent is compulsory for the Open House. However, if the report card is not taken, the parents will sign the acknowledgment and take care later, only on the dates specified
